Films I Love

Top 5 Films

Moana

via GIPHY

A young girl goes against her people's traditions to save her coastal island and people.

Really fun and I know all the songs by heart.

Encanto

Born in a family with magical powers, Maribel seems to be born without any.

I love the music in this one and the muscular, under pressured eldest daughter is a bit too relatable.

Descendants

via GIPHY

The children of Disney villians have been locked away with their parents. Now they are being allowed back into polite society.

Lots of great actors and singers. Not so great wigs. A good story about born evil vs made evil.

Wreck-It Ralph

via GIPHY

What happens when game characters no longer stay in their game?

I love the romance, action and cuteness in this.

Zootopia

via GIPHY

In a world where predators and prey live together, can a cop and a con artist stop a conspiracy that could tear their society apart?

Really funny film. An interesting take on fighting animal instincts.
